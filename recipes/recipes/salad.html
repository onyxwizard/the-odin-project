Avocado Salad

Description

This avocado salad is a delicious combination of creamy avocados, sweet and juicy tomatoes, bell pepper, cilantro, and fresh lime juice for an easy summer salad full of bold, fresh flavor and color.

Ingredients

  • Avocados: This recipe starts with two peeled, pitted, and diced avocados [2 ripe avocados]
  • Onion: A chopped sweet onion lends a bold flavor [1 sweet onion]
  • Bell pepper: A chopped green bell pepper gives the salad a welcome crunch [1 green bell pepper]
  • Tomato: A chopped ripe tomato adds even more color and flavor [1 large ripe tomato]
  • Cilantro: Chopped cilantro takes the flavor up a notch [¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro]
  • Lime: Fresh lime juice adds brightness and prevents browning [½ lime, juiced]
  • Seasonings: Simply season the avocado salad with just salt and pepper [salt and pepper to taste]

Directions To Cook

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine avocados, onion, bell pepper, tomato, cilantro, and lime juice in a large bowl.
  3. Gently toss until evenly coated. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Enjoy!
